Foreign Object Debris/Damage (FOD) prevention is a daily management practice during the fabrication and assembly process in order to deliver FOD free products.

FOD prevention is the responsibility of all personnel involved in the assembly, integration, test, manufacturing, storage, installation, operation, modification, delivery, process support, construction, transportation and maintenance of our products.
